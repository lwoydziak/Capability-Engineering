# RFC-001 Foundational Definitions

**Status: Draft**

## Purpose

Establish the foundational vocabulary used by
Capability Engineering.

## Definitions:
1. Knowledge
1. Behavior
1. Skill
1. Agent
1. Capability Asset
1. Capability
1. Capability Portfolio
1. Business Outcome

## Open Questions:

- Are Skills encoded Behaviors?
- Are Agents containers of Skills?
- Is Capability Asset a distinct concept?
- Can a Capability exist without Agents?
- What is the relationship between Capability
  and Process?
