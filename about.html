About

Building a shared language for enterprise capability

Capability Engineering is an emerging discipline focused on how organizations create, govern, transfer, and scale capabilities across humans, software, agents, and human-hybrid systems.

This project is currently being developed in public. The goal is not to publish a book, but to establish a shared language, validate foundational concepts, and evolve the discipline through discussion and critique.

If you work in enterprise architecture, AI platforms, transformation, organizational design, or technology strategy, your feedback is welcome.

This project is maintained by Luke Woydziak.

The public repository is available on GitHub.
